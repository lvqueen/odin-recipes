CHOCOLATE ICE CREAM

this is the best recipe I've ever done, and this is the most delicious dessert we have had the opportunity to prepare at home, that is easy and the best thing is you don't need too much ingredients

These are our ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs yolk, lightly beaten

Directions

  1. step 1 Combine milk, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to a simmer, stirring constantly.
  2. step 2 Place egg yolks into a small bowl. Gradually whisk in about 1/2 cup of hot milk mixture, then return to the saucepan. Heat and stir until thickened, but do not boil.
  3. step 3 Remove from the heat and stir in chopped chocolate until melted.
  4. step 4 Pour into a chilled bowl and refrigerate, stirring occasionally, until cold, about 2 hours.
  5. step 5 Stir in cream and vanilla. Pour into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's directions.
